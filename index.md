# Privacy Policy — Last Station Tycoon

**Last updated: July 2, 2026**

This privacy policy describes how **Last Station Tycoon** ("the game", "we") handles
your information. The game is developed and published by an independent developer
("the developer"). Contact: **marcostamoyofreire@gmail.com**.

## Summary

- The game contains **no advertising** and **no third-party ad networks**.
- Anonymous gameplay analytics are collected **only if you opt in** — the game asks on
  first launch, and you can change your choice at any time in **Settings → Privacy**.
- Purchases are processed entirely by **Google Play**; we never see or store your
  payment details.
- Your game progress is saved **locally on your device only**.

## Data we collect (only with your consent)

If you choose "Share anonymous usage data", the game sends anonymous gameplay events
through **Unity Analytics** (a service by Unity Technologies):

- Gameplay events: session start/end, tutorial progress, upgrades, hires, expeditions,
  purchases of in-game currency, and similar interactions.
- Technical data: device model, operating system version, country-level region, app
  version, and a random installation identifier (not tied to your identity).
- Crash and error diagnostics via **Unity's diagnostics service** (stack traces and
  device state at the moment of a crash), used solely to fix bugs.

We do **not** collect your name, email address, contacts, photos, precise location,
advertising identifiers, or any data used for tracking you across other apps or
websites.

Unity Technologies processes this data on our behalf. See Unity's privacy policy at
https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy for details on their processing.

## Data we do NOT collect

- No personal identification data.
- No advertising data (there are no ads in the game).
- No data from your device beyond what is described above.
- If you decline analytics (or never answer), **nothing is collected at all**.

## Purchases

In-app purchases (VIP pass, Energy Cell packs) are processed by **Google Play
Billing**. Google's handling of your payment information is governed by Google's own
privacy policy. The game only receives an anonymous confirmation that a purchase
succeeded, which is stored locally to unlock your items.

## Your choices and rights (GDPR / LGPD)

- **Opt in / opt out at any time**: Settings → Privacy → "Share anonymous usage data".
  Turning it off stops all collection immediately.
- **Data deletion**: to request deletion of previously collected analytics data, email
  **marcostamoyofreire@gmail.com** from any address with your request; we will forward
  the deletion request to Unity Analytics. Because the data is anonymous, we may need
  the installation identifier shown in Settings to locate it.
- **Local save data** is under your control: uninstalling the game deletes it.

## Children

The game is not directed at children under 13. It does not knowingly collect personal
information from anyone; analytics are anonymous and opt-in only.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the new version will be published at this same URL with an
updated date. Material changes will re-trigger the in-game consent prompt.
